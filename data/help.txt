#### HELP ####

--- Console ---
Default commands
  close  - close legekky
  reload - reload legekky
  go <ChannelName> - go to specified channel (Ex. go chat)
  cls - clear console (or something like that)
  vars - current values of variables

legekky modes
  tsun_mode - change tsundere mode
  troll_mode - change troll mode

--- Discord ---
